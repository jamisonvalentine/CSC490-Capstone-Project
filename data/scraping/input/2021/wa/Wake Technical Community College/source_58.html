EMS-241-4301 is restricted to Emergency Medical Science; Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, specific course meeting days for this blended class will be listed on the syllabus and posted to Blackboard by the instructor. Additional information may be provided by the instructor via email or Microsoft Teams. Due to the nature of emergency services, rotations are considered non-regularly scheduled clinical time. Students may attend clinical from 0700-1900, 1900-0700 or during peak times as scheduled by the EMS agency. Students will not be scheduled for any shift longer than 12 hours duration; however, late calls or emergencies may extend the scheduled shift. Each student will attend 16 shifts for a total of 192 clinical hours. Clinical orientation date 1/8/2021 (0900-1200).
